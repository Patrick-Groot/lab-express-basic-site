Some facts about his career:

  • In the year 2002 he joined Werder Bremen and stayed for 4 years
  • During that time they won the Bundesliga title, the DFB-Pokal and qualified for the Champion's League 3 times
  • With him they played the most beautiful and elegant football the world has ever seen
  • After his career he became a winemaker - probably great wine (a bit expensive though)!
